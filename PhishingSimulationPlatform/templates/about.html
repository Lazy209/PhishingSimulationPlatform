Project Objectives

  • Simulate realistic phishing email campaigns safely
  • Build a convincing fake login landing page
  • Track clicks and form submissions ethically
  • Never store actual user passwords at any point
  • Deliver post-click security micro-learning
  • Provide an admin metrics dashboard for evaluators

Problem & Solution

⚠ Problem

Phishing is the leading cause of initial access in cyber breaches. Employees often cannot recognize fake emails, spoofed domains, or urgent social engineering tactics until it is too late.

✓ Solution

PhishGuard provides a controlled training environment where users experience a simulated attack, metrics are collected ethically, and immediate education reinforces correct security behavior.
